Modular aluminum railing refers to a type of railing system that is made from aluminum components that can be easily assembled and disassembled in a modular fashion. It is a popular choice for residential and commercial applications due to its durability, low maintenance requirements, and aesthetic appeal.

LED aluminum railing is a modern and innovative architectural feature that combines the functionality of a railing system with the aesthetic appeal of LED lighting. It is commonly used in both residential and commercial settings to enhance the safety, visibility, and overall ambiance of the space.
